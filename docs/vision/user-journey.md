# User Journey — Hope-Core Real Estate Discovery

## North Star

> "We aren't just showing them what a house is worth today; we are showing them where their future makes sense."

## Target Persona

**The data-hungry investor-homebuyer:**
- Gen Z or millennial, not trust-funded
- Wants cap-rate math and price forecasts before making an offer
- Also wants to walk to a great coffee shop and live near peers (25–44 homeowners)
- Pessimistic about affordability but responsive to *evidence-based hope*

## Primary Journey (MVP)

### Step A: Set Intent

User lands on the DC metro map. Default view: **Opportunity Index** choropleth across 5 sandbox ZIPs.

User can:
- Toggle sidebar layers (investor vs hope-core metrics)
- See at a glance where green zones (opportunity) exist

*Psychological goal:* "Viable options exist — I just needed to see them mapped."

### Step B: Explore Neighborhoods

User clicks a promising ZIP (e.g., 22201 Arlington).

Map zooms to Level 2. Sidebar shifts to **Zip-level signals:**
- Left: Forecast & Valuation (forecast, PSF, overvaluation, market score)
- Right: Demographics & Hope-Core (remote work %, peer ownership %, walkability)

*Psychological goal:* "The math works here AND people like me are buying."

### Step C: Evaluate a Property (Phase 3)

User enters an address or listing URL.

System reveals **Offer Range:**
- Conservative: $292,000 (−16.55% vs list)
- Fair: $307,000 (−12.26% vs list)
- Competitive: $316,000 (−9.69% vs list)

With renovation adjustment pills and calculation breakdown.

*Psychological goal:* "I have a tactical edge — I know exactly what to offer."

## Future Journey (Phase 2 — Cinematic)

User scrolls. Camera descends from metro overview into neighborhood streets. Transit lines trace. Green spaces highlight. A locale quote fades in:

> *"It has better access to green and blue spaces than most big cities though."* — r/washingtondc

*Psychological goal:* "I can *feel* what living here would be like."

## Anti-Patterns (What We Are Not)

- **Zillow pin grid** — no financial foresight, pure pessimism
- **Spreadsheet-only investor tool** — no neighborhood soul
- **Vibe-only neighborhood guide** — no ROI credibility
